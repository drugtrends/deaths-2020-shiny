Drug-induced deaths defined by underlying cause of death (UCOD) codes

A Tabulation listof ICD-codes to identify causes of death attributable to drug-induced mortality was developed by the ABS based on a drug-induced death tabulation created by United States Centre for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). We have adopted these codes for our definition of all drug-induced deaths as given below.
