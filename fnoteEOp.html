These figures show opioid-induced deaths that were exclusively attributed to Heroin, opioid-induced deaths that were exclusively attributed to Other opioids (i.e. natural and semi-synthetic opioids, synthetic opioids), opioid-induced deaths that were attributed to both Heroin & other opioids, and the rest attributed to only Opium & unspecified opioids. Opioid-induced deaths are defined as deaths where opioid toxicity was considered to be the underlying cause.

Causes of death data undergo a revision process. Data for 2019 and 2020 are preliminary (indicated by 'x' in most of the plots) and subject to another two rounds of revision. Data for 2018 are revised (indicated by 'o' in most of the plots) and subject to another round of revision. Data for 2017 and earlier years are final. Data with number of deaths less than or equal to 5 have been suppressed (i.e. not presented). In accordance with recommendations to ensure stability of age-standardised rates from sparse data, age-standardised rates were not calculated if the total number of deaths was less than or equal to 10. Suppressed data are visible as gaps in the data series.

NOTE: Data unavailable for Intentional intent for Other and unspecified opioids.
